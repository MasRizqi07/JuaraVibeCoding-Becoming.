# Becoming: User Guide

Welcome to **Becoming.** This AI-powered experience takes you on a deeply introspective journey to map your core identity and project your potential futures.

## How to Use This App

### 1. The Landing Page
When you arrive, you will experience a sequenced intro text setting a contemplative mood. Click "Enter" to begin.

### 2. Sign In
To keep your data secure, sign in with your Google account. All sessions are private to you.

### 3. The Reflection Flow
You will be asked 8 questions:
- **Questions 1–5:** Core, foundational questions about your ambitions and fears.
- **Questions 6–8:** Dynamically generated adaptive questions by our AI, reacting on the fly to your previous answers.
Take your time and answer honestly. The more detail you provide, the deeper the final analysis will be.

### 4. AI Processing
Once you finish, the system analyzes your responses using an advanced cognitive reasoning model. Wait for a few moments as your results are generated. (Observe the dynamic phases while it loads).

### 5. Results Dashboard
Your reflections generate a comprehensive dashboard with 6 main tabs:

- **Overview:** See how you rank in categories like 'Execution', 'Resilience', and 'AI-Era Readiness' via a visual Radar Chart.
- **Future Split:** See a sobering view of "Future A" (if you stagnate) compared to an inspiring "Future B" (if you fulfill your potential).
- **Identity:** Review your core identity archetype, strengths, and blind spots. You can click on specific traits to have the AI generate short, punchy explanations and tactical advice in real-time.
- **Letter:** Read a customized "Letter from the Future" written by a successful future-version of yourself, to you today.
- **Plan:** Tangible execution plans. This includes a Learning Roadmap, Weekly Habits, and an Anti-Procrastination Protocol.
- **Share:** Generates a clean, minimalistic "Identity Card" that you can customize with an AI-generated tagline and download as a PNG.

### Giving Feedback
In the top right corner of the results dashboard, there is a **Feedback** button. If you spot a bug or have a feature suggestion, let us know!

## FAQ
**Is my data private?**
Yes. Your responses and results are saved securely and strictly scoped to your authorized Google account.

**Can I view past sessions?**
Currently, each new reflection session overwrites the generated result so it reflects who you are *now*. We recommend downloading your Share Card for record-keeping.
